Book 1The Infinity Pool
January 12, 2021
Reviewed years after reading

This book offers a deep exploration of its subject, weaving together historical insight, narrative clarity, and analytical depth. Across its chapters, the author carefully builds a framework that connects abstract ideas to tangible examples.

One of the book’s greatest strengths lies in its structure. Each section leads logically into the next, reinforcing previous ideas while introducing new material with precision.

The author balances theory and application skillfully, leaving the reader with insights that endure well beyond the final chapter.
